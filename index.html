auto-awesome by mtodd

A Turntable.fm Bookmarklet for Automatic Awesome

For when you're in your office and want to have the Awesome button automatically hit for you!

Dependencies

You may need to look up the unique DOM ID of your awesome button before clicking the bookmarklet. You can do this by "Inspecting Element" and copying the ID. It's different for every person, and probably every time. However, we do try to look it up so you don't have to.

Install

Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmark bar! AutoAwesome

For instructions on how to use the AutoAwesome bookmarklet, Thad Williams wrote up a nice visual guide!
